Cheeseburger

Here is a recipe for a cheeseburger that I enjoy

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Bun

Instructions

  1. Form the ground beef into patties.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or stovetop until desired doneness.
  3. Add cheese to the patties and let it melt.
  4. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, and any other toppings you like.
