Lasagna

Ingredients

Description

I've always loved lasagna as a kid, and I believe the best cooks are the ones that create good lasagna. Here is a recipe that I found of it.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil
  • Fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, parsley, and a pinch of salt.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil.
